Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RCE Labs Limited ("we", "us", or "the Company") handles information in connection with the mobile application RCE GameGrid (the "Application" or "Service").

About the Application

RCE GameGrid is a multi-game mobile application featuring: Whot, Gem Crush, Word Hunt, Memory Match, and Color Match.

Data Collection

We do not require users to create accounts and we do not directly collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

RCE GameGrid uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC, to display advertisements within the Application.

Google AdMob may collect and process certain data automatically to serve ads, including but not limited to:

  • Device information (such as device model, operating system version)
  • Advertising ID
  • IP address
  • General location information
  • App interaction data

This data collection is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy. We encourage users to review Google’s privacy practices at:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Personalized Ads

AdMob may use data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, depending on user settings and regional regulations. Users can control ad personalization through their device settings.

Local Data Storage

The Application may store non-personal information locally on your device, such as game progress, high scores, and settings. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Children's Privacy

RCE GameGrid is family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. However, advertisements served by Google AdMob may involve data processing as described above. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided personal information should contact us.

Data Security

While we do not directly collect personal data, we rely on trusted third-party services such as Google AdMob. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of these providers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a revised "Last updated" date.
